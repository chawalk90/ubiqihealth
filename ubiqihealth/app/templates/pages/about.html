ABOUT THE COMPANY

Klio Health was founded by experienced entrepreneurs, designers, and engineers who saw that people dealing with chronic conditions do not have good ways of determining whether treatments or lifestyle changes are actually effective. We help the millions with chronic conditions and their care providers efficiently track symptoms and treatments. We approach this in a standardized, engaging manner that saves both time and improves the quality of care.
