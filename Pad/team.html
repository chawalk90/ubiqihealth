The Team

Padibility originated among a group of friends at Harvard University who met in the same dorm entryway during their freshman year. Their stressful summer housing searches prompted a simple question: what if the same data-driven process that matched us at Harvard could be made available to countless others?

Naji Filali

Naji is the Executive Team Manager and Header of Operations and Outreach to real estate firms. He's tasked with building a network of trusted brokerage.

Tim Hopper

Tim is the Chief Strategist and Marketing Manager. He manages outreach efforts to potential consumers through existing campus-based social networks.

Angie Peng

Angie is the Market Research Manager. She has experience in management consulting and finance and specializes in consumer behavior analysis.

Charles Walker

Charles is the Junior Web Developer. He is tasked with designing and maintaining the web site.
