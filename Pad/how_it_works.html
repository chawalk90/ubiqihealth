How it works

Padibility recognizes that finding a place to stay should not and cannot occur independently of finding a person to room with. Padibility uses a data-driven compatibility matching algorithm to match those with "pads" and are looking for roommates with those seeking them, and vice-versa:

  1. Fill out a brief questionnaire about yourself and your ideal or current pad.
  2. Receive recommendations and browse the profiles of pad seekers or pad owners who best fit your needs.
  3. Once you are interested in discussing the possibility of living with someone, send them a Request. They will be notified of your invite and upon acceptance, you can chat further.
  4. Confirm your roommate selection or Pass and keep on browsing!"
