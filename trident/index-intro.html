Boston's Last Independent General Bookstore

Since 1984, Trident Booksellers & Cafe has been providing Back Bay residents, area students, and passing tourists with sustenance for the mind and body. With free wifi, an award-winning magazine selection, full service restaurant and new and discount books, Trident is the perfect place to catch up with friends, study for your exam grab brunch or just browse the bestseller.

Check out the Trident’s extensive Menu, featuring an extensive fresh juice and smoothie menu as well as coffee, espresso, and a variety of loose leaf teas. Be sure to like us on Facebook to keep up with what’s happening at the Trident!

Visit our Online Store to purchase gift cards, Trident gear, MEM Tea and Rao’s Coffee!
